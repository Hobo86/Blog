MySQL:常用命令行

Oct 30th, 2013

登入 mysql -h192.168.1.110 -uroot -ppassword

登出 quit/exit

查看数据库 show databases;

用户权限 添加 grant select on db.table to ‘user’@‘host’; grant select,update on . to ‘test’@‘%’; 撤销 revoke all on . from ‘test’@‘%’; 查看 show grants; show grants for user@localhost

删除用户 delete from mysql.user where user=‘’ and host=‘’;

设置密码 update mysql.user set password=PASSWORD(‘123456’) where user=‘root’;

配置远程连接 GRANT ALL PRIVILEGES ON . TO ‘root’@‘%’ IDENTIFIED BY ‘password’ WITH GRANT OPTION;
